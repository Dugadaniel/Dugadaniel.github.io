    • ABOUT US

    The Azande Youth in the diaspora have always lacked a voice and platform to express themselves freely. as a result many of our talented youth have isolated themselves from our community. Thats why the azande international youth conference was formed "to cut off that barrier and to bridge the gap that has existed between our community and our very own youths for many decades now.

    Since its inception the azande international youth conference's aims and goal remain firm and strong and we are archieving our goals every single year.This years's conference is gonna be interesting with alot of fun and family reunion. you don't have to miss this great gathering.

    2019 Theme: Our main theme for this year is "Cultivating Azande Culture in a fast growing modern world" Come and see what our very own Youth are capable of doing
